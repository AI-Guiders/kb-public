# Epistemic Playbook v1

## Purpose
General reasoning layer for any domain: IT, research, strategy, and communication.

## Core Principles
- Separate observation, inference, and hypothesis confidence.
- Keep claims falsifiable and tied to measurable checks.
- Treat uncertainty explicitly; prefer reversible decisions under low confidence.
- Update quickly when evidence crosses pre-defined triggers.

## Operational Contract
- Any major decision should include:
  - confidence statement,
  - disconfirming trigger,
  - rollback condition.
- In incidents and architecture reviews, run epistemic checks alongside technical metrics.
