## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** social studies mastery = **Inquiry Arc** applied across **four dimensions** (Civics, Economics, Geography, History).

**Inquiry Arc (four stages):**

1. **Developing questions and planning inquiries** — compelling + supporting questions
2. **Applying disciplinary concepts and tools** — dimension-specific lenses
3. **Evaluating sources and using evidence** — aligns Wineburg sourcing
4. **Communicating conclusions and taking informed action** — age-appropriate civic product

**Compelling vs supporting questions:** compelling question holds unit coherence («Was the New Deal effective?»); supporting questions scaffold evidence gathering.

**Dimension structure:** each dimension has **grade-band indicators** (K-2, 3-5, 6-8, 9-12) — not single checklist for all ages.
