## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** Students overwhelmingly **fail** basic online evaluation (who's behind info, evidence, other sources) — **Civic Online Reasoning** teaches fact-checker heuristics (lateral reading, click restraint).

| National portrait (2021) | Finding |
|--------------------------|---------|
| n | 3,446 HS students, live web tasks |
| Failure modes | Trust «look» of site, weak sourcing, no lateral verification |
| Demographics | Scores vary by grade, SES, locality — **instruction needed**, not innate |
| Curriculum | COR modules; DIG reports trained > control on assessments |

**Three questions (COR):** Who's behind the information? What's the evidence? What do other sources say?
