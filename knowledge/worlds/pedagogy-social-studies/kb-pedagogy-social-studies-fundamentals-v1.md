# Social studies pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.social-studies`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School social studies: civics, economics, sociology basics, inquiry from sources, informed action. Out of scope v1: partisan messaging without method.

---

## North star

**C3 inquiry arc:** develop questions → apply disciplinary concepts → evaluate sources → communicate conclusions → take informed action.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-social-studies-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-social-studies-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| S1 | Compelling questions | refine → supporting questions |
| S2 | Disciplinary concepts | civics/econ/geo/history lens |
| S3 | Source evaluation + argument | claim–evidence–reasoning |
| S4 | Informed action (age-fit) | plan tied to inquiry |
