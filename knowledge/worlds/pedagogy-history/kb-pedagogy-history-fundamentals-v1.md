# History pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.history`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School history: historical thinking, primary sources, argument, periodization. Out of scope v1: archival research methods at grad level as gate.

---

## North star

**Historical thinking:** students **read sources critically**, contextualize, corroborate, and build causal arguments — not memorize chronology alone.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-history-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-history-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| H1 | Sourcing | who/when/why/purpose of document |
| H2 | Contextualization | situate in time/place norms |
| H3 | Corroboration | compare accounts, resolve tension |
| H4 | Causal / historical argument | claim with evidence, avoid presentism |

---

## School labels (authoring only)

Древний мир, Новое время, Отечественная история — **section tags**, not gate oracles.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Timeline quiz as sole mastery
- Single narrative textbook without sources
- Opinion essay without document evidence
