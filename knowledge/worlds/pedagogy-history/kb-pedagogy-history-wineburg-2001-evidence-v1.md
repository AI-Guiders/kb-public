## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** historical thinking is **unnatural** — novices read past as present; experts **source, contextualize, corroborate**.

**Four operational skills (SHEG/Wineburg):**

| Skill | Question expert asks |
|-------|---------------------|
| **Sourcing** | Who made this? When? Why? |
| **Contextualization** | What was going on when this was created? |
| **Corroboration** | Do other sources agree/disagree? |
| **Close reading** | What does this source actually say (vs assume)? |

**Expert–novice finding (1991 strand):** historians read **skeptically by default**; students often **credulous**, especially with textbook tone.

**SHEG assessments:** document-based items with forced choice + short explanation — e.g. «Which source more useful for X?» requiring sourcing rationale.
