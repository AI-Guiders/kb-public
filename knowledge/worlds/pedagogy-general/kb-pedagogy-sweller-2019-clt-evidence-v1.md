## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** human **cognitive architecture** (working memory limits, long-term schema storage) implies **instructional effects** that remain robust 20 years after original CLT synthesis.

**Effects catalog (2019 update):**

| Effect | Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|--------|
| **Worked example** | Novice overload on problem-solving | Study examples first; fade guidance |
| **Split-attention** | Text + diagram separated unnecessarily | Integrate labels or spatially align |
| **Redundancy** | Same info in slide text + narration | Remove duplicate channel |
| **Modality** | Visual WM loaded | Audio for some explanations — avoid redundant text |
| **Expertise reversal** | Advanced learners slowed by heavy guidance | Replace examples with problems |
| **Guidance fading** | Permanent scaffolds | Systematic removal |
| **Element interactivity** | High-interactivity tasks need more support | Chen et al. strand — match support to complexity |

**Biologically primary vs secondary knowledge:** primary (e.g. general problem-solving heuristics we evolved) may need **different** instruction than secondary (school-curated domains like algebra) — secondary benefits most from explicit instruction + worked examples.
