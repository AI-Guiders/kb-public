## Elkonin (1974–1984) — stages of literacy (summary)

| Stage | Content | CASA authoring hint |
|-------|---------|---------------------|
| 1 | Phonemic orientation **before** letters | bootstrap analytic read — **temporary** |
| 2 | Vowel phonemes + letters | vis/text bind vowels |
| 3 | Consonants + anticipatory reading | target **whole-word fluency** (SVR) |

**Sources:** Elkonin D.B. *How to Teach Children to Read*; Elkonin–Davydov method handbook (LBZ open PDF where available).
