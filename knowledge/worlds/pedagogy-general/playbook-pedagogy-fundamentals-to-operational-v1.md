# Fundamentals → Operational playbook v1

**world:** `pedagogy.general`  
**When to open:** создание/правка curriculum, probe spec, subject world.

## Purpose

Зафиксировать порядок **evidence → fundamentals → playbook → authoring** до любого lab claim.

## Core contract

1. Прочитать `status-pedagogy-school-subjects-v1.md` § DoD.
2. Cross-cutting evidence: `kb-pedagogy-scientific-evidence-v1.md` → cards.
3. Subject: subject status → subject scientific-evidence index → **one** paper card.
4. Fundamentals subject file (`status: draft` | `active`).
5. `matrix-pedagogy-cross-subject-transfer-v1.md` if ≥2 subjects or L1 link.
6. Public authoring checklist: `playbook-kb-world-public-authoring-v1.md`.
