## Fundamentals

**Core claim:** solving problems while learning can impose **high cognitive load** and impede schema acquisition; **studying worked examples** can be superior for novices.

**Load types (later formalized; seeds here):**

- **Intrinsic** — element interactivity of task
- **Extraneous** — poor instructional design
- **Germane** — resources devoted to schema construction (terminology refined in later CLT)
