# Visual arts pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.visual-arts`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School visual arts (ИЗО): studio habits, making, critique, reflection. Out of scope v1: professional atelier certification, art market expertise.

---

## North star

**Studio Thinking:** develop **habits of mind** through structured studio practice — observe, envision, express, reflect, stretch & explore, understand art worlds.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-visual-arts-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-visual-arts-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| A1 | Studio habits (develop craft, envision…) | artifact + habit reflection |
| A2 | Critique discourse | describe → interpret → evaluate |
| A3 | Making cycle | draft → revise with intent |
| A4 | Art worlds awareness | connect work to context |

---

## School labels (authoring only)

Рисунок, живопись, композиция, история искусства — **section tags**, not gate oracles.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Copy masterwork as sole assessment
- Grade only final product, ignore process
- Art history dates without visual analysis
