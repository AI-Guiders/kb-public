## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** Quasi-experimental arts education research shows **some** competency benefits but evidence is **sparse, small-N, heterogeneous** — avoid instrumental hype; support **intrapersonal/interpersonal + discipline habits**.

| Honest read | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Positive signals | Some music/visual domains → specific subskills |
| Limits | Few gold-standard RCTs; effect sizes vary widely |
| Policy | ESSA/Wallace 2023: arts integration modest avg. achievement bump — **not** replacement for code/literacy gates |
| Hetland layer | Studio Thinking = **habits of mind** framework; Schneider = **outcome evidence audit 2021** |
