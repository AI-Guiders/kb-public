## Fundamentals

Meta-analysis of **systematic phonics** vs unsystematic/no phonics (38 studies, 66 treatment–control comparisons, peer-reviewed, post-1970). Primary outcome: pooled reading at end of training or end of Year 1.

**Headline:** overall **d = 0.41** (moderate); persists at follow-up (d = 0.51 immediate → **0.27** delayed, still > 0).

## Operationalization (RU written v1)

- **Wave 0–1:** systematic print phonics early (align Castles K7–K8); held-out **nonword + word** probes
- **Gate must include comprehension** even when phonics strong (K16); older struggling readers need RC strategy + vocab if D adequate (K19–K20)
- **Do not expect** phonics alone for LA with cognitive limitations (K11) — different tract
- **Delivery:** class instruction can match tutoring effect sizes in corpus — scale for von Neumann cohort OK if quality held
- **Fade:** after full alphabetic phase, shift emphasis per Ehri 2014 / Castles §2–§3

## Limitations

- Published studies only (possible publication bias — authors argue unlikely given effect distribution)
- English programs; irregular orthography
- Moderator causality tentative (confounding)
