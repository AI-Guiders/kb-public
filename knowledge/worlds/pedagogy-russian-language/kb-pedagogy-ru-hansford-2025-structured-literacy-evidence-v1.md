## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** 2025 analysis finds **structured literacy** (systematic phonics, scope/sequence, decodables) effect sizes **~identical to NRP 2000** (d≈.44–.46); **balanced literacy** weaker (d≈.19–.29).

| Program type | Weighted ES (approx.) | Notes |
|--------------|----------------------|--------|
| Structured literacy | .46 | Matches 20-year phonics meta stability |
| Balanced literacy | .19–.29 | Improved vs old whole-language but << SL |
| Comprehension | SL decoding win; comprehension needs **additional** vocabulary/knowledge instruction |

**Honest limits:** exploratory design; rigor moderators reduce ES in strict RCTs — aligns with Castles «code necessary not sufficient».
