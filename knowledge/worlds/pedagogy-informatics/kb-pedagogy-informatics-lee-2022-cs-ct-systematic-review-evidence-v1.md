## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** **66-study systematic review** — CS education in K-12 **promotes computational thinking** and creative/critical thinking; early access + varied approaches recommended; not all interventions equally successful.

| Recommendation | Rationale |
|----------------|-----------|
| Early CS access | Wing (2008) CT-for-all echoed |
| Multiple pedagogies | Unplugged, games, robots, integrated curriculum — mixed evidence base |
| Integration | CS in other subjects can ↑ motivation/participation |
| Honest caveat | Implementation quality varies — framework alone insufficient |

**Stack:** Lee 2022 (field snapshot) → K12CS 2016 (standards) → PRIMM 2021 (program text pedagogy).
