# Aviation Human Factors Reading Map v1

## Objective

Build a safety-focused, evidence-aware understanding of aviation psychology from first principles to operational controls.

## Track A - First-Party Standards and Guidance

- ICAO Human Factors Training Manual (Doc 9683)
- ICAO CRM/TEM guidance material
- FAA ADM and pilot human-factors handbooks
- EASA CRM and human-factors implementation guidance

## Track B - System Causation and Error Models

- Reason's layered defense model (Swiss-cheese framing)
- HFACS-style classification (organizational -> supervision -> preconditions -> acts)
- Accident-chain interpretation limits (avoid single-cause bias)

## Track C - Safety Culture and Reporting

- Just Culture principles
- Occurrence reporting frameworks (mandatory + voluntary channels)
- Non-punitive reporting boundaries (gross negligence / wilful violation exception)

## Track D - Field Observation and Proactive Capture

- LOSA methodology and no-jeopardy observation principles
- TEM coding in normal operations
- Converting observation into training and SOP updates

## Ingestion Protocol

For each source:
- extract one operational claim,
- define observable signal,
- define countermeasure,
- define transfer boundary,
- tag confidence level.
