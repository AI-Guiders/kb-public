# Biology pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.biology`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School biology: phenomena, systems, evolution, ecology, structure–function. Out of scope v1: medical licensing content, grad-level molecular detail as gate.

---

## North star

**Three-dimensional learning:** integrate **science practices**, **crosscutting concepts**, and **disciplinary core ideas** in coherent storyline units.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-biology-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-biology-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| B1 | Phenomenon anchoring | explain unfamiliar case |
| B2 | Practices (model, argue, investigate) | claim–evidence–reasoning |
| B3 | Crosscutting (systems, scale, cause) | transfer to new system |
| B4 | Core ideas depth | not glossary breadth alone |

---

## School labels (authoring only)

Ботаника, зоология, анатомия, экология — **section tags**, not gate oracles.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Label-matching worksheets as mastery
- Skip practices; teach definitions only
- One-shot lab demo without explanation demand
