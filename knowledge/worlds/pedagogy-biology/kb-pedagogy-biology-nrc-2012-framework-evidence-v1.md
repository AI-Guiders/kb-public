## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** K-12 science proficiency requires **three integrated dimensions** (Box S-1):

1. **Science and Engineering Practices (SEPs)** — what students *do* (investigate, model, argue from evidence, …)
2. **Crosscutting Concepts (CCCs)** — lenses across domains (patterns, cause/effect, systems, energy/matter, …)
3. **Disciplinary Core Ideas (DCIs)** — limited set of big ideas per domain (life, physical, Earth/space, engineering)

**Learning progression logic:** progress is **coherent understanding of ideas over time**, not checklist of facts; practices and crosscutting concepts **support** core idea development.

**Engineering design** included as parallel strand (define problem → optimize solution under constraints).
