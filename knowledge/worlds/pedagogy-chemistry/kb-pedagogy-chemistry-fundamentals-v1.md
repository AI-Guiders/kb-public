# Chemistry pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.chemistry`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School chemistry: particulate models, reactions, stoichiometry sense, inquiry labs. Out of scope v1: research synthesis routes, industrial scale-up.

---

## North star

**Particulate + inquiry:** learners explain observable changes with particle-level models; active structured inquiry beats passive coverage.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-chemistry-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-chemistry-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| C1 | Particulate reasoning | diagram → macro prediction |
| C2 | Symbolic literacy | equation with meaning, not algebra trick |
| C3 | Inquiry / POGIL cycle | data → claim → model revision |
| C4 | Lab sensemaking | explain anomaly, not fill table |

## Anti-patterns

- Recipe lab without conceptual target
- Memorize periodic trends without model
