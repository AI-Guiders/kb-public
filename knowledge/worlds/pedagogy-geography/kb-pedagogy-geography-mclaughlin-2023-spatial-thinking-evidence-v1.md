## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** Spatial skills **benefit** learners but get **insufficient classroom practice** — interventions cluster around mapping, sketching, models, gestures; need **longitudinal spatialization** of curriculum.

| Theme | Examples in review |
|-------|-------------------|
| Intervention types | Sketching, gestures, physical/computer models, curricular spatial units |
| Skills | ~half **intrinsic** spatial skills in coded studies |
| Gap | Process of spatial reasoning under-studied at **secondary** |
| Edelson bridge | Three I's (interactive, interpretive, investigative) need **spatial practice** layer |
