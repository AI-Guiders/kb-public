# Geography pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.geography`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School geography: geo-literacy, spatial thinking, human–environment interactions, regional systems. Out of scope v1: professional GIS certification content as gate.

---

## North star

**Geo-literacy:** understand **interactions** (human–environment), **interconnections** (systems across places), **implications** (decisions and futures) — with spatial reasoning.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-geography-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-geography-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| G1 | Spatial thinking | map ↔ phenomenon translation |
| G2 | Interactions | explain process in place |
| G3 | Interconnections | trace cross-border/system link |
| G4 | Implications | decision with geographic consequence |
