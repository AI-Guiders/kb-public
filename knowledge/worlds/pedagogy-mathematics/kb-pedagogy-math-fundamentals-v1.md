# Mathematics pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.mathematics`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School mathematics: number sense, procedures, representations, problem solving. Out of scope v1: competition olympiad tactics, university proof courses.

---

## North star

**Procedural + conceptual balance:** fluency on held-out items **and** explain/represent transfer, not speed drill alone.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-math-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-math-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| M1 | Number / quantity sense | novel context, same structure |
| M2 | Procedural fluency | timed + untimed held-out |
| M3 | Representations (CRA) | concrete → pictorial → symbolic fade |
| M4 | Problem solving | multi-step, unfamiliar surface |
