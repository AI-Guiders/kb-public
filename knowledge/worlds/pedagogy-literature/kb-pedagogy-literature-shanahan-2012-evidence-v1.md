## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** **Disciplinary literacy (DL)** ≠ generic «content area reading strategies» — each discipline has **specialized norms** for text, evidence, and production.

**Shanahan contrast table (conceptual):**

| Discipline | Expert reading emphasis |
|------------|-------------------------|
| History | Sourcing, contextualization (see Wineburg) |
| Science | Model-based text, procedural precision |
| Mathematics | Dense symbolic + word problem translation |
| **Literature** | **Form, craft, theme, intertextuality** — aesthetic and argumentative readings |

**Literature-specific:** experts attend to **authorial choices** (structure, figurative language, voice), construct **interpretive claims** supported by textual evidence, tolerate **ambiguity** more than single-right-answer STEM items.
