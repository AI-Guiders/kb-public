# Literature pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.literature`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School literature: close reading, genre, argument from evidence, composition about texts. Out of scope v1: graduate critical theory canon as gate.

---

## North star

**Disciplinary literacy:** teach **how literary texts work** — structure, craft, interpretation — with text-evidence reasoning, not plot recall alone.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-literature-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-literature-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| L1 | Close reading | cite line → infer → explain |
| L2 | Text structure / genre | compare structures across texts |
| L3 | Argument from evidence | thesis + textual support |
| L4 | Craft awareness | device → effect on meaning |

---

## School labels (authoring only)

Поэзия, проза, драма, устное народное творчество — **section tags**, not gate oracles.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Retell plot as mastery
- Author biography trivia gate
- Generic «find metaphor» without effect explanation
