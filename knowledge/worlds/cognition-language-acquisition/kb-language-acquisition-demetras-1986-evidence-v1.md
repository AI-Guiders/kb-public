## 1. Устойчивое извлечённое знание

| # | Тезис | Детали из статьи |
|---|--------|------------------|
| **K8** | **Explicit** feedback (approval/correction) **редок** и **не** дифференцирует WF vs IF utterances | ~15% adjacent responses = explicit approval (A); ~3% = explicit correction (C). Cs equally or **more** likely after WF; As somewhat more after IF |
| **K9** | **Implicit** feedback **дифференцирует** WF vs IF: MO после WF, CQ после IF | Во всех 4 dyads predominant response к WF = **move-on (MO)**; в 3/4 к IF = **clarification question (CQ)**. Conditional probability: CQ → higher P(IF) |
| **K10** | Implicit negative feedback **многочастее** explicit | ~26% adjacent maternal = CQs vs ~3% explicit C; sequences of implicit feedback frequent, explicit sequences rare/absent |
| **K11** | **Repetitions (R)** типологически различимы: Exact-R чаще после WF; Ext-R/Con-R чаще после IF | Exact-R ≈ explicit approval function; Exp-R/Con-R/Ext-R несут corrective information |
| **K12** | Explicit feedback направлен на **semantic content / truth value**, не на form | Подтверждает Brown & Hanlon (1970); exception: approval of «please» |
| **K13** | Judges **не** могли надёжно атрибутировать implicit responses к phonological vs syntactic vs semantic source (кроме obvious phonology) | Ambiguity breakdown → child interpretation also noisy |
| **K14** | Non-adjacent responses included — важно vs Brown & Hanlon / Hirsh-Pasek et al. | «Turn exchange» unit; sequences after first maternal response analyzed |
| **K15** | Sample: 4 college-educated mothers, 2;0 children (Alex, Amy, Ben, Jim); home play/eating | MLU 2.2–3.5; N small, homogeneous — authors warn generalization |

**Discussion (authors):** implicit negative/positive feedback **exists** and is produced differentially; learnability axiom «no negative feedback» **ill founded** for these dyads — но utility **suboptimal** (proportional, not categorical; negative feedback to WF utterances occurs). **Не** causal claims.

---

## 2. Метод (суть)

- Child utterances: WF / IF / Other (laughs, vocalizations).
- Maternal: explicit (A/C) + implicit (R, CQ, MO). R subtypes: Exact, Contracted, Expanded, Extended. CQ: wh-, yes/no, occasional, repetition-with-rising-intonation.
- Coding: breath-group maternal utterances; rising intonation → CQ not R.

---

## 3. Ограничения

- N=4; causality not tested.
- Explicit feedback ≠ «grammar correction» in everyday sense.
- Debate with Bohannon & Stanowicz (1988) and learnability models remains open.
