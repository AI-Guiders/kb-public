## 1. Устойчивое извлечённое знание

| # | Тезис | Где в книге |
|---|--------|-------------|
| **K14** | Lexicon **central** — without words, no syntax/phonology inventory to organize | ch. 1 |
| **K15** | **Conventionality:** for a meaning, speakers expect a **conventional form** in the community; forms are arbitrary but **consistent** across occasions | ch. 4; def. also in ch. 3 mapping section |
| **K16** | **Contrast:** difference in form → assumed difference in meaning; speakers do not tolerate **complete synonyms**; repairs in word-search underline priority of «right word» | ch. 4 |
| **K17** | **Mapping problem:** child must isolate forms, construct meanings, map meanings→forms using ontological categories + lexical constraints | ch. 3 |
| **K18** | Pragmatic bootstrapping: **Conventionality + Contrast** apply to **every** mapping attempt; other constraints (basic-level, mutual exclusivity, whole-object) hold in **limited domains** and must be narrowed | ch. 3–4 |
| **K19** | **Context / shared knowledge:** novel words and deictics need situational cooperation; addressee uses Gricean contract to infer non-conventional senses | ch. 4–5 |
| **K20** | **Lexical innovations** fill **gaps**; must **contrast** with existing forms; Clark & Clark (1979) contract for innovative denominals etc. | ch. 5 |
| **K21** | On acquiring new word: children **drop overextensions** of old words for the same domain (e.g., stop calling cats «dog» when «cat» learned) — contrast in action | ch. 5, Table 5-1 repairs |
| **K22** | **Pre-emption:** conventional form blocks innovation for same meaning | ch. 4, Tables 4-2, 4-3 |

**Operational implication (human domain):** disambiguation «какое слово» = **referent + conventionality + contrast**, not phonological prefix collapse.

## 2. Операционные определения (Clark)

- **Conventionality:** «For certain meanings there is a form that speakers expect to be used in the language community.»
- **Contrast:** «Speakers take every difference in form to mark a difference in meaning.»
- **Transparency / simplicity / productivity:** guide **word formation** and innovations (Part 2 case studies) — separate from but coupled to conventionality/contrast.
