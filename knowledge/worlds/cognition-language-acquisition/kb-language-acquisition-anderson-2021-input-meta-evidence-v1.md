## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** Meta-analysis — **input quality** (vocabulary diversity, syntactic complexity) predicts child language **more strongly** than raw quantity (tokens/utterances).

| Effect (pooled r) | k | N |
|-------------------|---|---|
| Quality | .33 | 1,958 |
| Quantity | .20 | 1,411 |

**Moderators:** naturalistic contexts > free-play tasks; quality effects ↑ with child age and observation length; not moderated by SES/gender in pooled model.
