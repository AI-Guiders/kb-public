## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** **High-intensity** active learning (≥⅔ class time) improves outcomes for all and **narrows URM achievement gaps** vs traditional lecture — low-intensity active ≈ lecture.

| Outcome | Finding |
|---------|---------|
| Exam scores | Active learning ↑ performance; gaps ↓ **33%** (Bayesian IPD, 15 studies, n≈9,238) |
| Failure rates | Gaps ↓ **45%** (26 studies, n≈44,606) |
| Intensity moderator | Only **high-intensity** active closes gaps meaningfully |
| Heads-and-hearts | Deliberate practice + **inclusive** teaching needed for equity gains |

**2020+ stack for von Neumann physics:** Theobald (why IE + intensity) → Wells (how: modeling cycle) → Hake (historical FCI anchor).
