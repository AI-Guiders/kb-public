# Physics pedagogy — fundamentals v1

**Мир:** `pedagogy.physics`  
**Версия:** v1.0 · 2026-06-06 · **status:** draft

---

## Scope

School physics: phenomena, models, representations, inquiry labs. Out of scope v1: advanced mathematical physics, competition problem sets as sole gate.

---

## North star

**Modeling + conceptual change:** learners revise naïve models using structured tasks and representations, verified on held-out contexts.

Evidence: [`kb-pedagogy-physics-scientific-evidence-v1.md`](kb-pedagogy-physics-scientific-evidence-v1.md).

---

## Four pillars (v1)

| Pillar | Construct | Probe idea |
|--------|-----------|------------|
| P1 | Phenomenon anchoring | predict–observe–explain |
| P2 | Representations (graphs, vectors, diagrams) | translate across reps |
| P3 | Model refinement | misconception-targeted item |
| P4 | Quantitative reasoning | unit-aware, not symbol plug-in |

---

## School labels (authoring only)

Механика, термодинамика, электричество — **section tags**, not gate oracles.

---

## Anti-patterns

- Definition recitation as mastery gate
- Lecture-only sequence without interaction
- Math procedure green imported as physics understanding
