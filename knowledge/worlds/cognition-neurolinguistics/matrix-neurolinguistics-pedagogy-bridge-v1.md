## Operational rules

1. **Pedagogy card wins** on «what to teach Monday»; neuro card wins on «why not neuromyth X».
2. Cite **both** Hartshorne (grammar in immersion-like learners) **and** Genesee (school programs) before age-cutoff policy.
3. Neural dual-route **confirms** behavioral dual-route — does not replace Ehri OM operational tags.

## Red flags

- «VWFA lit up → skip phonics»
- «Age 17 cutoff → cancel secondary EN program» (misread Hartshorne)
- «Bilingual brain separate slots → grammar translation OK»
