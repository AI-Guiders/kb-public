## Fundamentals

**Thesis:** word-specific **VWFA** responses appear when children **begin reading**, not from maturation alone; pre-readers already show **functional connectivity** VWFA ↔ left language network — literacy **recycles** this plastic hub.

**Design:** 3 groups (French): 6yo pre-readers, 6yo beginning readers, 9yo advanced readers — words, faces, houses, checkerboards fMRI.

| Finding | Detail |
|---------|--------|
| Word selectivity | **Absent** pre-reading; **present** in beginning readers (age-matched) |
| Face selectivity (FFA) | Slow age-driven maturation; schooling not primary driver |
| Pre-reading connectivity | VWFA site already linked to **left spoken-language** areas; FFA to contralateral/amygdala |
| Face vs reading | No direct pruning of face responses; **indirect** — reading slows left-hemisphere face growth → stronger right bias |

**Dehaene (2023 Collège de France; Reading League 2024):** same recycling + GPC teaching principles — **stable** with this developmental data.
