# 30-minute onboarding

Goal: enter a working rhythm in half an hour without extra calls.

## 0–5 minutes: context

1. Open [home](../index.md).
2. Read [Entry KB](../knowledge/00-entry-kb-v1.md).
3. State in one sentence: why KB exists in this environment.

## 5–12 minutes: working loop

1. Read [router](../knowledge/index-knowledge-router-v1.md).
2. Fix your personal step template:
   - entry,
   - boundary,
   - exit,
   - verification.

## 12–20 minutes: memory and recovery

1. Read [Memory and KB](../guide/memory-and-kb.md).
2. Locate where you keep:
   - hot context,
   - stable protocols,
   - archive tail.

## 20–25 minutes: communication

1. Read [protocols](protocols.md) (WORK/HUMAN modes).
2. Agree on a switch phrase and soft reject for urgency.

## 25–30 minutes: entry check

Answer in writing:

1. What is the source of truth for decisions?
2. What if you lost context?
3. When does a note go to KB vs not?
4. How do you switch WORK/HUMAN correctly?
5. What is your next minimal verifiable step?

If all five are clear, onboarding is done.
