# Glossary

## KB

Knowledge Base: durable memory of decisions, protocols, and operational rules.

## Hot context

Minimal data needed right now to continue work.

## Return anchor

Short stop record: where you stopped, what is next, what blocks.

## Recovery

Action sequence to restore focus after a breakdown.

## WORK

Execution-first mode: decisions, checks, closing loops.

## HUMAN

Relational-first mode: attention to state, nuance, contact quality.

## Soft reject

Temporary deferral of a HUMAN request due to urgency, with mandatory return.

## First principles

Baseline causal rules explaining why the system is built this way.

## Definition of Done

Criteria for a step to be complete and verified.

## Source of truth

Authoritative place for a given knowledge type.
